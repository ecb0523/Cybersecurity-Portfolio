# Compliance Audit & Risk Assessment: Botium Toys

## Scope
An end-to-end security audit of Botium Toys’ physical and digital assets, focusing on internal processes and regulatory compliance.

## Compliance Findings
- **PCI DSS:** Noted that credit card data must be encrypted and access restricted to authorized personnel.
- **GDPR:** Verified the 72-hour breach notification plan and the need for stricter data classification.
- **SOC Type 2:** Identified gaps in user access policies and data integrity validation.

## Recommendations
- Implement **Least Privilege** across all systems.
- Establish a **Disaster Recovery Plan** and centralized **Password Management System**.
- Deploy an **Intrusion Detection System (IDS)** and secure legacy systems with a regular maintenance schedule.
